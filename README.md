# Senior Product Designer Portfolio

## Hello There! 👋🏽

Hi, and welcome to the codebase for my portfolio! Maintaining a portfolio is hard work, isn't it? I've tried numerous approaches to this problem, and in time reasoned that it was utterly unnecessary where a deck often sufficed.

Unfortunately, the design market's a bit different now, and if I want to put my best foot forward, I can think of no better way to do that than with a portfolio. Besides, it's a great way to show off my coding chops, establish a stable presence online (RIP Twitter), and to signal to others what I'd like to do next.

Previously, I've created my portfolio on a self-hosted instance of WordPress, and after that, on Jekyll. After much deliberation on whether to stick with static site generators or go back to the world of databases and CMS's, I've decided to use Eleventy for this iteration.

Thanks for taking a look, and if you notice any errors or typos, I welcome your feedback. Cheers! 🥂✨
